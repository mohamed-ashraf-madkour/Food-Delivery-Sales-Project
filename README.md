# 🍽️ Food Delivery Sales Analysis

A data analysis project exploring restaurant sales performance, customer ratings, and ordering trends across multiple cities and states in India.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



# 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes food delivery and restaurant order data to uncover sales trends, customer preferences, and regional performance insights.

The analysis focuses on:
- Revenue trends over time
- Best-performing cities and states
- Customer rating behavior
- Order distribution patterns
- Restaurant and category performance

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


# 🎯 Project Goals

- Analyze total sales and order activity
- Track monthly, weekly, and daily revenue trends
- Identify top-performing cities and states
- Explore customer rating patterns
- Compare sales performance across regions
- Build KPI-driven restaurant insights

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



# 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



# 📂 Project Features

## 📊 KPI Overview
Provides a high-level summary of business performance.

### Includes:
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Ratings
- Revenue Performance

### Key Insight:
The dataset shows strong order activity across multiple regions with consistent customer ratings.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



## 📈 Revenue Trend Analysis
Tracks sales performance over time.

### Includes:
- Monthly revenue trends
- Weekly revenue analysis
- Revenue by day of the week

### Key Insight:
Revenue patterns fluctuate across weeks and months, highlighting changing customer ordering behavior.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



## 🌍 Geographic Analysis
Analyzes restaurant performance across cities and states.

### Includes:
- Top 5 cities by sales
- Top 10 states by sales
- Regional sales comparison

### Key Insight:
Certain metropolitan cities contribute significantly more to total sales compared to other regions.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



## ⭐ Customer Rating Analysis
Explores customer satisfaction and rating behavior.

### Includes:
- Average restaurant ratings
- Rating count analysis
- Rating trends across categories

### Key Insight:
Most restaurants maintain relatively high customer ratings, indicating strong customer satisfaction levels.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



## 🍛 Restaurant & Category Insights
Analyzes food categories and restaurant performance.

### Includes:
- Category-level sales analysis
- Restaurant sales contribution
- Popular dishes and order behavior

### Key Insight:
Popular food categories and highly rated restaurants drive a large portion of overall sales.
